Getting your Birth Certificate

No ID? No worries! We've figured out the easiest starting point for you.

Estimated Time: Lucky for you, this can be completed in as little as 20 minutes!

There are TWO things you'll need:

  1. Fill out the SS-5 Application form
  2. 3 Forms of Identification

If you are in Anchorage, this is your nearest Birth Certificate office. Print out your form and take your submit documents in person.

Let's Keep Going!

FAQ: What is the fastest way to get a certified copy of a certificate?
The fastest way to obtain a certificate is to go to a Vital Statistics office located in Anchorage or Juneau. If filled out correctly, many requests can be processed while you wait. However, some requests may take up to 48 hours to complete. Please note that not all certificates are available at the Anchorage office. (Source) http://dhss.alaska.gov/dph/VitalStats/Pages/faqs.aspx
