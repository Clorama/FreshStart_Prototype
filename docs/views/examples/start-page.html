Fresh Start: Identification

This section will walk you through:

  • how to get your government issued IDs
  • what documents you'll need and how to get them
  • Where to submit your applications for your IDs
  • you'll need your IDs to be readily available for hire by employers

Filling out the required applications can take up to 30 minutes.

After submitting all of your documents, you can receive your id within two weeks. It will be a piece of cake!
